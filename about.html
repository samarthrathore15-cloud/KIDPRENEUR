About Kidii

Kidii is a student-led community that empowers young innovators to share startup ideas, learn skills, and debate real-world problems.

Share an ideaBrowse ideas

Mission & Vision

Home

Our mission is to give students a safe, creative space to convert curiosity into action. We believe in hands-on learning, peer feedback, and building an inclusive startup mindset early.

Vision: A global community where student ideas are visible, debated and supported — where the next generation of changemakers can prototype and test solutions together.

How it started

2019
Idea day
A mini-hackathon at school kicked off the idea: kids want a stage for their startup ideas.
2021
Community growth
More students joined, and we launched the first submission flow and peer-review threads.
2024
Kidii platform
Now an easy-to-use web app for submitting ideas, debating and iterating — with plans to add mentorship & judging.
